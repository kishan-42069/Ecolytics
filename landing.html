The Problem

Your footprint matters more than you think

Climate change isn't just a government problem. Individual choices drive 45% of all emissions — yet most people have no idea what their actual number is.

Most people emit 4× the safe limit
The science-backed target is under 2.3 tonnes per year. In developed nations, the average is 8–16 tonnes — without people realising it.
Small changes compound dramatically
One fewer beef meal per week. One fewer flight per year. Taking the bus twice a week. Together, these can cut your footprint by 30–50%.
You need data before you can act
You can't manage what you can't measure. Ecolytics gives you an honest, personalised number — across transport, energy, food, and shopping.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is Ecolytics really 100% private?
Yes. Ecolytics has no backend database. All your logs, scores, and settings are stored entirely in your browser's local storage. If you clear your browser data, your Ecolytics data is deleted. We cannot see your data.
How are my emissions calculated?
We use standard emission factors provided by global institutions like the IPCC, US EPA, and India's MoEFCC. For example, when you log miles driven, we multiply that by the regional average carbon intensity for a passenger vehicle.
Is it free to use?
Yes! Ecolytics is 100% free and open-source. There are no premium tiers, no hidden fees, and no ads. Our goal is climate action, not monetization.
Do I need to make an account?
No. You don't need an email or a password. You can start tracking your footprint instantly the moment you open the app.
